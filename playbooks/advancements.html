üìã Advancement Examples

Here are a few examples of the types of moves available in the Advancements Handout:

Enhanced Senses

You gain heightened senses that allow you to perceive things others miss. Take +2 on Notice rolls when using your enhanced senses.

Quick Reflexes

Your reflexes are supernaturally fast. Once per scene, you can automatically succeed on a defense roll against a physical attack.

Social Charm

You have an innate charm that makes people like you. Take +2 on Rapport rolls when trying to persuade or influence others.

Minor Magic

You can perform minor magical effects. Choose one type of minor magic (illusions, telekinesis, elemental control, etc.).
