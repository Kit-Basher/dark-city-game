Playbooks are your character classes in Dark City. Each playbook represents a different type of supernatural being or extraordinary individual. When you create a character, you choose one playbook and gain access to its main moves plus one subtype specialization.

Each playbook contains multiple moves - choose three from your main playbook and/or your chosen subtype to start your character's journey.
