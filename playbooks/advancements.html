üìñ How Advancements Work

Character advancements are special moves that any character can take when they level up, regardless of their original playbook. These are balanced versions of playbook-exclusive moves that have been adapted for general use.

Key: -x- = Playbook exclusives | -x-a- = Playbook exclusive with advancement version | -a- = Advancement version of a playbook move

Using Advancements:

  • When your character levels up, choose one new advancement move
  • These moves work alongside your existing playbook moves
  • Some advancement moves have special requirements or conditions
  • Advancement moves allow characters to diversify beyond their original playbook
